    History of Colectivo

    Colectivo is a Milwaukee based coffee house that ethically sources, roasts, and brews its own coffee. From humble beginnings to a full blown opperation, Colectivo has completely found a home in Milwaukee.

    Mission and Values

    Colectivo is more than just a coffee shop and we strive to deliver more than just a great coffee experience. From developing close partnerships with coffee farmers and cooperatives around the world to creating inviting environments at every Colectivo cafe. We as a neighborhood cafe aspire to bring communities together.
